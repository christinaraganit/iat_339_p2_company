About Us

A collaborative design project based in Vancouver, Canada focused on exploring the potential of connection through design. Drawing inspiration out of the city we’re from, including music, art, and performance, we aim to deliver high quality limited merchandise, unique designs, and one of a kind experiences.

Our Story

Dayside was founded by a group of passionate individuals who shared a common vision: to create a brand that resonates with the essence of the city we’re from. Our journey began with the idea that fashion should be more than just a trend; it should be a way of life. Inspired by the simplicity and elegance of nature, we set out to create a brand that celebrates the culture that defines it.
